FIRST CALL FOR PAPERS

Special Track/Sessions on

Artificial Immune Systems and Their Applications
at
IEEE International Conference on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics(SMC'2000)
Nashville, Tennessee, October 8-11, 2000.

A special track on Artificial Immune Systems and Their Applications will be organized (third time) at the IEEE International Conference on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics. This interdisciplinary track will serve as a forum for presenting and disseminating current research in the field of Artificial Immune Systems. The aims are to strengthen this emerging research area by exploring different immunological mechanisms and their relation to information processing and problem solving.

The scope of this special track/sessions include the following topics(but are not limited to):

  • Computational methods based on Immunological principles
  • Artificial Immune systems for Pattern Recognition
  • Immunity-based system for Anomaly or fault detection
  • Immune Network Models and their applications
  • Immunity-based system as a multi-agent system
  • Immunogenetic approaches
  • Immunity-based systems for self-organization
  • Immunity-based approach for collective intelligence
  • Immunity-based systems for optimization and search
  • The immune system as Autonomous Decentralized Systems
  • Immunity-based approach for Artificial Life
  • Immunological approach for computer security
  • Immunity-based systems as a distributed learning system
  • Immunological Computation for data mining
  • Immunity-based Cognitive Models
    •
